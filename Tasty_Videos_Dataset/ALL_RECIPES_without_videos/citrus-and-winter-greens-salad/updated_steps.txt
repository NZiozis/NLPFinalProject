To make the dressing, add the garlic, parsley, scallions, apple cider vinegar, honey, orange juice, Dijon mustard, tahini, and salt to a blender.
Blend until smooth.
With the blender running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the dressing is easily pourable.
Set aside until ready to use.

Toss well to combine.
