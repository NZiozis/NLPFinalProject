Make the spiced seeds and nuts: In a small bowl, combine the hazelnuts, nigella seeds, sesame seeds, cinnamon, turmeric, crushed coriander, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, ground ginger, salt and pepper.
Mix together and set aside for garnish.
These seeds and nuts will keep in an airtight container for up to 1 week.
Make the soup: Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium hight heat.
Add the carrot, onion, celery, parsnip, garlic, salt, and pepper.
Stir and cook until the vegetables are fully softened, about 10 minutes.
Add the broccoli, asparagus, and vegetable broth.
Bring to a boil.
add the lemon juice, kale, spinach, and stir to wilt the greens in the hot soup.
Do not cook the greens for longer than about 3 minutes.
Once the greens have wilted, puree the soup to your desired consistency.
Serve immediately.
Top with coconut cream, chia seeds, avocado, and the spiced seeds and nuts.
Enjoy!
