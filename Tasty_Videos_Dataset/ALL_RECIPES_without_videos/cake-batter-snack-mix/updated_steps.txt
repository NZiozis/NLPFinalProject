In a medium bowl, mix coconut oil and white chocolate.
Melt in microwave for 60 seconds.
Stir every 15 seconds until melted.
Place cereal in large bowl and add extract into melted chocolate.
Mix in melted chocolate and sprinkles then stir until evenly coated.
In a large plastic bag, add powdered sugar and the cereal mix.
Close and shake until each cereal is coated with sugar.
Enjoy!
