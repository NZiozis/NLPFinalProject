Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
Place the squash and zucchini onto a baking tray.
Evenly coat with olive oil, salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
Spread squash and zucchini to the sides and place chicken sausage in the middle.
Bake for 15 minutes, or until zucchini and squash are cooked through.
Serve now with wild rice, or refrigerate in airtight container up to 3-4 days.
Enjoy!
