Add the cucumber, eggplant, okra, Japanese ginger, shiso, soy sauce, mirin, vinegar, and sugar to a bowl and gently mix.
Heat a pan over medium heat and add the chicken and sake.
Cook until no longer pink and cooked through.
Add salt, black pepper and green onion.
Lightly fry, then set aside.
Place lettuce on one side of the tortilla, then top with chicken, 1 tomato, and cheese.
Top with the cucumber and eggplant mixture and fold over.
Enjoy!
