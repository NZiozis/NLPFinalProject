Heat the oil in a medium pot over medium heat until it reaches 350˚F (180˚C).
In a medium bowl, combine the flour, cornflour, baking soda, and sugar.
Whisk well.
Add the milk and whisk to combine.
Add the vanilla and whisk to incorporate.
Dip the apple slices in the batter, letting the excess drip off.
Fry the battered apples in the hot oil, 2 at a time, until golden brown on both sides.
Drain on paper towels.
Repeat with the sliced bananas, chocolate bars, and chocolate sandwich cookies.
Dust the dessert pakoras with powdered sugar and drizzle with honey.
Serve with strawberry and chocolate syrup for dipping.
Enjoy!
