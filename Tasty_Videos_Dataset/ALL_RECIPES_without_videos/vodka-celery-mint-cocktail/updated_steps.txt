Add the celery and mint in a pestle and mortar.
Crush until the celery juices are released and you have a mushy mixture.
Add ice cubes to a glass and add the vodka, simple syrup, lime juice.
Place the celery and mint mixture into a sieve.
Using a spoon press over the glass to push all the fresh celery juice through the sieve.
Top with soda water and finish with a sprig of mint and a wedge of lime.
Enjoy!
