Whisk together egg spinach, red pepper, salt and pepper.
Heat a nonstick pan over a medium heat.
Place a greased mason jar lid right side up on the pan.
Slowly pour egg mixture into lid.
Cover, cook for 3 minutes
Remove cover.
Using a fork pull the lid off of the egg.
Flip egg, cook for another 3 minutes.
Remove from heat once cooked through.
Place egg patty on English muffin and top with sliced avocado.
Enjoy!
