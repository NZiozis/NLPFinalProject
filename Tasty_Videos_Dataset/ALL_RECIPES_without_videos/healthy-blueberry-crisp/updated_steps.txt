Preheat oven to 350˚F/180˚C.
Combine dry ingredients of the crisp topping in a bowl.
Then add in the wet ingredients and mix until evenly coated.
Add blueberries to a medium-sized baking dish, then cover with the crisp topping.
Bake 45-50 minutes, or until golden brown and blueberries start to bubble.
Mix together Greek yogurt and maple syrup in a measuring cup.
Drizzle over your blueberry crisp.
Serve and enjoy!
