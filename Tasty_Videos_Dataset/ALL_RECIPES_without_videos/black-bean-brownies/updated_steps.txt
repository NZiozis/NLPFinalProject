Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C).
Add black beans to a food processor and pulse until blended.
Add eggs, baking powder, unsweetened cocoa powder, coconut oil, maple syrup, and vanilla extract.
Blend until well combined.
Spray loaf pan with cooking spray or wipe down with oil.
Pour mix into a baking pan.
Sprinkle dark chocolate chips on top.
Bake for 30-35 minutes.
Enjoy!
