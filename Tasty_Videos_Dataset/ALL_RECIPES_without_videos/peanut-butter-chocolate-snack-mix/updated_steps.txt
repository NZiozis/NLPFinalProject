In a medium bowl, mix chocolate and peanut butter.
Melt in microwave for 60 seconds.
Stir every 15 seconds until melted.
Place cereal in large bowl.
Add extract into melted chocolate then mix with cereal and stir until evenly coated.
In a large plastic bag, add powdered sugar and cereal mix.
Close and shake until each cereal is coated with sugar.
Add in desired candies.
Enjoy!
