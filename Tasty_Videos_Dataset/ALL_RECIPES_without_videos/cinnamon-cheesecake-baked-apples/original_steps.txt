Mix cream cheese, and sugar. Mix in the egg, vanilla extract, and cinnamon.
Hollow the apple with a melon baller.
Fill ⅔ of hollowed apple with cream cheese mixture.
Bake 25-30 minutes at 176˚C (350˚F).
For maple pecan sauce, boil whipping cream and maple syrup until thick. Stir in pecans just before serving.
Serve with crushed graham cracker (optional) and maple pecans sauce.
Enjoy!
