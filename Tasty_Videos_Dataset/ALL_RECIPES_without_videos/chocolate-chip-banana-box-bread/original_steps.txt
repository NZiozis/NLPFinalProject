Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C). Line two 9x5-inch (23x13 cm) loaf pans with parchment paper and grease with nonstick spray.
In a large bowl, mash the bananas.
Add the eggs and vegetable oil and whisk until smooth.
Add the cake mix and chocolate chips and stir with a spatula to combine.
Split and pour the batter into two loaf pans lined with parchment paper and nonstick baking spray.
Bake for 30 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean when inserted in the middle.
Cool before slicing and serve.
Enjoy!
