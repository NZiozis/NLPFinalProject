Cut each cinnamon roll into 8 pieces.
In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon.
Set it aside.
Pour the butter on the bottom of a 9 X 13 inch (22cm x 33cm) glass baking dish.
Spread the cinnamon roll pieces evenly over the butter.
Then pour the mixture over the cinnamon rolls.
Pour 1 cup of maple syrup over the mixture.
Bake at 375°F/190°C for 25 min.
Top with the icing from the cinnamon rolls.
Enjoy!
