Preheat oven to 350°F/180°C.
In a bowl, mix chicken, broccoli, alfredo sauce, and ½ cup/55 grams of mozzarella.
Stuff mixture into the jumbo shells, and place in rows in a greased 9x13 inch/23x33 cm glass baking dish.
Spread on ¼ cup/57 grams alfredo sauce and sprinkle on remaining ½ cup/55 grams of mozzarella.
Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
Enjoy!
