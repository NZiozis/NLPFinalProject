Boil the garbanzo beans until the skins come off.
Remove the skins, drain, and combine all ingredients in a food processor.
Blend until smooth adding small amounts of oil and water until desired consistency is achieved.
This recipe usually requires more liquid, at the potato will absorb a lot! Add more lemon or tahini if needed.
Add fresh veggies or pita chips sprinkled with cinnamon!
Enjoy!
