In a large mixing bowl, combine peanut butter, powdered sugar, vanilla, and rice cereal.
Mix well and freeze.
Roll out prepared brownie and cut into 32 squares (8 rows of 4).
Using a melon baller or your hands, form peanut butter mixture into size of a truffle.
Form brownie square around mixture and seal it around the peanut butter ball.
Dip brownie in melted chocolate, covering the entire ball.
Using a spoon, drizzle warm peanut butter over the brownies and chill.
Enjoy!
