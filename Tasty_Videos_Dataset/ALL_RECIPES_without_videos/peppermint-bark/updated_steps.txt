Place the mini candy canes in a zip top bag and use a rolling pin to crush them into small chunks.
Transfer to a medium bowl.
In a separate medium bowl, stir the peppermint extract into the melted chocolate chips.
Pour onto a parchment paper-lined baking sheet and spread evenly with a spatula.
Freeze for 5 minutes.
Take the pan out of the freezer and pour the melted white chocolate over the chocolate, spreading evenly with a spatula.
Sprinkle the crushed mini candy canes over the white chocolate.
Freeze for at least 1 hour.
Remove the bark from the freezer and break into pieces.
Enjoy!
