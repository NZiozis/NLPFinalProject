In a blender, combine the banana, oats, peanut butter, cocoa powder, shredded coconut, maple syrup, vanilla, and milk.
Blend on high speed for 3 minutes, or until completely smooth.
Transfer the chocolate coconut mixture to a small saucepan and heat over medium-low heat until just warmed through.
Do not boil.
Pour into a mug and serve immediately.
Enjoy!
