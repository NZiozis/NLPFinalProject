Slice chicken breast into strips.
Add the curry powder, oil, pepper, and salt to the prepared chicken.
Rub in the dry ingredients and make sure the chicken is evenly coated.
Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
Add the chicken and cook until partially done.
Remove from skillet and place in a clean bowl.
Add onions, ginger, and garlic to skillet.
Cook until the onions are translucent.
Add the diced tomato, curry powder, cayenne pepper, and chicken stock.
Return the partially cooked chicken.
Stir and cook until chicken is done.
Stir in the chicken stock cornstarch slurry.
Cook until the curry has thickened.
Turn off the heat and stir in the Greek yogurt.
Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C), place french fries on baking sheet, bake for 18 minutes (either from scratch or frozen).
Pour chicken curry over french fries, garnish with cilantro, as desired.
Enjoy!
