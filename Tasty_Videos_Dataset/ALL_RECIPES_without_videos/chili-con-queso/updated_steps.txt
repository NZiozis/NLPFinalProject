Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
Add the onion and bell pepper and cook for 2 minutes, until starting to soften.
Add the ground beef and chili seasoning.
Cook until the beef is cooked through, about 5 minutes.
Remove the pan from the heat.
In a small saucepan, whisk together the milk and heavy cream.
Bring to a boil, then remove from the heat.
In a small bowl, whisk together the water and cornstarch to form a slurry.
Pour the slurry into milk mixture and whisk to combine.
Put mixture back on the heat and bring to a simmer.
Whisk in the cheese until fully melted and season with the salt.
Add the diced tomatoes and green chiles and whisk to combine.
Pour the queso over the chili mixture.
Garnish with scallions and serve with corn chips for dipping.
Enjoy!
