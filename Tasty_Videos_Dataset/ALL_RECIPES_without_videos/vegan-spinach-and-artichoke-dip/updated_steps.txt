Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C).
Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
Add garlic and saute for 1 minute, stirring frequently.
Reduce heat to medium-low heat and add spinach.
Cook until spinach is wilted.
Add tofu, nutritional yeast, lemon juice, basil, salt, and pepper to a blender or food processor.
Pulse until smooth.
Add artichokes and spinach mixture, and pulse until well-incorporated.
Transfer to greased baking dish and bake for 30 minutes.
Remove from the oven and serve with chips, crackers, or bread slices.
Enjoy!
