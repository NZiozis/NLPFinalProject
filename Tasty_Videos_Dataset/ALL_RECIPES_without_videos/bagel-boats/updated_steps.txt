Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
In a small bowl, mix cheese, bacon, and green onion.
In a small bowl, whip 2 eggs with milk.
Carefully hollow out 1 bagel with a small silver spoon.
Place both bagel halves on a baking sheet.
Fill both bagel halves with cheese and egg mixtures.
Bake in the oven for 25 mins, or until the egg is cooked.
Enjoy!
