Shred carrots by hand or in a food processor then set aside in a small bowl.
In a large bowl combine brown and powdered sugars with eggs, whisk until smooth.
Add oil and mix.
Add remaining carrot cake ingredients and stir until blended, set aside.
In a large bowl, whisk cream cheese and powdered sugar until smooth to start the cheesecake.
Mix in eggs one at a time.
Add in remaining ingredients and mix until well blended.
In a greased 8-inch (20 cm) springform pan, pour ¾ of the carrot cake mixture and smooth it out, followed by ½ of the cheesecake mixture, then repeat with remaining mixtures.
Bake in for 45 minutes at 180°C (350°F).
Remove from oven and let cool to room temperature, about 1 hour.
Refrigerate for 4 hours, or overnight.
Enjoy!
