Spray the inside of a microwave-safe ceramic mug.
Add in flour, cocoa powder, sugar, and baking powder.
Mix to combine.
Add the vegetable oil, milk, and stir until smooth.
Stir in the chocolate chips.
Microwave on high for 1 minute 30 seconds.
Let cool completely.
Top with whipped cream, sprinkle on fresh blueberries, raspberries, and mint.
Enjoy!
