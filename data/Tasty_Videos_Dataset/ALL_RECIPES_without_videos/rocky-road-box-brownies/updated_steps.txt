Prepare the brownie mix batter according to package instructions.
Add nuts to your brownie mix batter.
Mix thoroughly.
Pour into baking pan.
Bake the brownie mix according to package instructions.
Remove from the oven, and top with marshmallows.
Broil for 30 seconds, until the marshmallows are toasty.
Serve with a drizzle of chocolate syrup.
Enjoy!
