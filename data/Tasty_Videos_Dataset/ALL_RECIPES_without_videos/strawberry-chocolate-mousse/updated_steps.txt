In a small saucepan, heat milk on medium-low heat until scalding around 180˚F (82˚C).
Do not boil the milk.
Pour hot milk over chocolate.
Add vanilla and salt.
Let it stand for 3 minutes to soften.
Whisk together until fully incorporated.
Let cool.
Add yogurt.
Whisk together until fully incorporated.
Layer chocolate in the bottom of 2 cups followed by strawberries.
Repeat until the glasses are full.
Enjoy!
