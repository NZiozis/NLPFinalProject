In a medium bowl, mix cream cheese and Nutella.
Set aside.
Roll out puff pastry sheet on a lightly floured surface.
Divide into 9 equal squares.
Scoop 1 Tbsp.
of Nutella filling onto the corner of each puff pastry square.
Leave enough space for the edges to be crimped shut.
Fold the square to form a triangle, crimp the edges shut.
Set the turnovers on a lightly greased cookie pan.
Prepare the egg wash by beating together one egg and 1 Tbsp.
water.
Brush the top of each turnover with the egg wash.
Bake for 350˚F (175˚C ) for 25 minutes, or until golden brown.
Allow to cool.
