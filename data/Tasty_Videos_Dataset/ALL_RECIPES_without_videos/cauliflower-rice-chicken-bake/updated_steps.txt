Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
In a large bowl add the cauliflower rice, broccoli, chicken, mozzarella, cheddar, garlic powder, onion powder, and salt.
Stir well to combine.
Add mixture to a baking pan then cover with aluminum foil.
Bake for 40 minutes.
Uncover then top with remaining cheddar.
Bake for 10 minutes.
Remove and allow to cool for 5-10 minutes.
Garnish with parsley, if desired and serve.
Enjoy!
