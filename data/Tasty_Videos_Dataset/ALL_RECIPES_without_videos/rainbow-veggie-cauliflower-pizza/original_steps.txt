Preheat oven to 450˚F (230˚C).
Place cauliflower pieces into bowl of food processor and pulse for 30 seconds, or until cauliflower is the texture of cornmeal.
Transfer cauliflower to a microwave-safe bowl and microwave on high for 4 minutes. Let cool.
Transfer the cooked cauliflower into a clean dish towel and wring out the excess water.
Discard water.
Place cauliflower into a bowl. Add oregano, basil, garlic powder, sea salt, red pepper flakes, and egg. Mix thoroughly by hand.
Transfer cauliflower mixture onto a greased parchment-lined pizza tray or cookie sheet.
Pat out the dough into a disk approximately ¼-inch thick.
Bake for 8-10 minutes. Remove from oven.
Spread a layer of pizza sauce on the crust.
Add cheese (optional).
Top with your favorite rainbow vegetables.
Bake for 8- 10 minutes, or until the spinach is wilted and the cheese is melted.
Enjoy!
