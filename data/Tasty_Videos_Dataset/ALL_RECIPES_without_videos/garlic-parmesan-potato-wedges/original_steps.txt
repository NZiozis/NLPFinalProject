Preheat oven to 375ºF (190ºC).
Slice potato in half then take the half and quarter it.
Whisk 3 eggs in a small bowl.
Combine remaining ingredients in a medium bowl.
Coat each wedge in egg wash, then coat in dry mix, until entirely covered.
Place on a greased baking sheet and bake for 30 minutes.
