Preheat oven to 325˚F (160˚C).
In medium bowl, add coconut flakes, soy sauce, maple syrup, and liquid smoke.
Mix until thoroughly coated.
Grease pan with coconut oil.
Spread the coated coconut flakes in a thin layer onto a baking sheet.
Bake for 10-15 minutes, flipping every five minutes until golden brown.
Serve with your favorite dish.
Enjoy!
