Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
In a bowl, mix shredded chicken with ½ cup (115) barbecue sauce.
Set aside.
Spread pizza dough out on a parchment-lined pan.
Bake 10 minutes.
Top with remaining barbecue sauce, cheddar, chicken, mozzarella, red onion, and parsley.
Bake an additional 10 minutes.
Slice and dip!
Enjoy!
