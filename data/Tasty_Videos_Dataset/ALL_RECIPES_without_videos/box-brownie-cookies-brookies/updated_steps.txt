Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C).
Combine the brownie, eggs, and oil until a sticky dough forms.
Take an even amount of cookie dough and brownie dough and roll them into a golf ball-sized sphere.
Repeat with the remaining dough.
Place 6 balls on a parchment paper-lined baking sheet.
Bake for 14 minutes.
Enjoy!
