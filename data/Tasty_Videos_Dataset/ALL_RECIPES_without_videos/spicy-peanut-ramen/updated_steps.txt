To a pot with 2 cups (480 ml) of boiling water add the ramen seasoning packet and a tablespoon of butter.
Stir until the butter melts and then add the ramen noodle cube and cook until the noodles become tender, 2 minutes.
Pour the ramen into a bowl.
To the prepared ramen, add the peanut butter, Sriracha, and sesame oil and stir to incorporate.
