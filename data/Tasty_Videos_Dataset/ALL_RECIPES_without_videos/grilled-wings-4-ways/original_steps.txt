Fiery
Heat grill to high heat. Place wings on the grill and close lid. Grill for 8–10 minutes, then flip and grill for another 8–10 minutes (with the lid closed), or until wings are fully cooked through.
Remove wings from grill. Squeeze with lime wedges and garnish with minced cilantro.
Zesty
Heat grill to high heat. Place wings on the grill and close lid. Grill for 8–10 minutes, then flip and grill for another 8–10 minutes (with the lid closed), or until wings are fully cooked through.
Remove wings from grill. Squeeze with lemon wedges and garnish with basil leaves.
Jazzy
Heat grill to high heat. Place wings on the grill and close lid. Grill for 8–10 minutes, then flip and grill for another 8–10 minutes (with the lid closed), or until wings are fully cooked through. Remove wings from grill and set aside.
In a small pot over low heat, melt the butter. Add honey, apple cider vinegar, and salt and bring to a simmer. Remove from heat.
Drizzle grilled wings with honey butter.
Savory
Heat grill to high heat. Place wings on the grill and close lid. Grill for 8–10 minutes, then flip and grill for another 8–10 minutes (with the lid closed), or until wings are fully cooked through. Remove wings from grill and set aside.
Drizzle grilled wings with herb butter.
Enjoy!
