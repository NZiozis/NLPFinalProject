Make the sauce: Combine the sour cream, lime juice, cumin, paprika, salt, pepper, and cilantro in a mason jar.
Stir with a fork until smooth and well-combined.
Add the rice, black beans, corn, tomato, chicken, cheese, and cilantro.
Store in the refrigerator for 2-3 days.
When ready to eat, shake well to combine.
Enjoy!
