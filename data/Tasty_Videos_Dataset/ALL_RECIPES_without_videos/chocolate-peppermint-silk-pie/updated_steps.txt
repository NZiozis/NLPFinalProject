Preheat oven to 425˚F (200˚C).
Place a prepared pie crust into a 9-inch (23 cm) pie pan.
Line a sheet of parchment paper over crust and place 4 spoons in pan or dried beans in on the parchment, weighing down the crust.
Bake for 15 minutes.
In a medium sized heat-safe mixing bowl, mix eggs and sugar until combined.
Bring bowl over pot filled with water on low heat and rest on top.
Constantly beat egg mixture on low speed unit it reaches 160˚F (77˚C).
Remove the bowl from the heat and stir as it cools.
Add peppermint extract and melted chocolate.
Stir until combined.
Gradually beat in cubes of butter, stirring until combined.
Gradually fold in whipped cream and mix until just incorporated.
Carefully spoon filling into prepared pie crust and chill for at least 2 hours.
Garnish with whipped cream, shaved chocolate, and crushed peppermints.
Enjoy!
