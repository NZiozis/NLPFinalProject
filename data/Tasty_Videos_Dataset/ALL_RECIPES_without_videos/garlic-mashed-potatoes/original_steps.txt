In a medium bowl, add the potatoes and garlic and stir until evenly combined.
Microwave for 13 minutes. Remove from the bowl from the microwave.
In a liquid measuring cup or small bowl, combine the milk, salt, and butter and stir to combine.
Pour the milk mixture over the potatoes.
Microwave for 2 minutes.
Remove from the microwave and mash the potatoes with a fork.
Add the pepper and stir to incorporate.
Garnish with chives.
Enjoy!
