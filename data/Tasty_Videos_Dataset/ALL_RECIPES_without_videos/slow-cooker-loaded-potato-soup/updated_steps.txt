Add the potatoes, onions, chicken broth, salt and pepper to the slow cooker.
Set it to low for 6 hours or high for 4 hours.
In a saucepan, combine the butter, flour, heavy cream and sour cream.
Whisk until thickened.
When the slow cooker is done, pour in the thickened mix.
Stir, cover and set the slow cooker to low for another 30 minutes.
Serve with shredded cheese, bacon and chopped scallions.
Enjoy!
