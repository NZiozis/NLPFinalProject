Mash banana in a large bowl and then add in the oats, honey, vanilla, cinnamon, and a pinch of salt.
Fill a greased muffin tin with the granola mixture and use a spoon to form the crust.
Let chill for 1 hour.
Preheat oven to 350˚F (180˚C) and bake for 30 minutes. Let cool.
Add in Greek yogurt to each cup and fill with toppings.
Enjoy!
