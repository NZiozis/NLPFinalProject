Drain corn and pour into slow cooker.
Add milk, sugar, and pepper.
Stir until mixed.
Without stirring- place the thinly sliced butter and cubed cream cheese on top.
Cover and cook on high for 2-3 hours.
Stir until butter and cream cheese are mixed in.
Cover again for 15 minutes.
Garnish with thyme and serve.
Enjoy!
