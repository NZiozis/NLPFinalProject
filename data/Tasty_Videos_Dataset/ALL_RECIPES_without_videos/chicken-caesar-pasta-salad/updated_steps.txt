Boil water, add the pasta, and cook according to package instructions.
Drain water, and let the pasta chill.
Meanwhile, season each of the chicken breasts with 1 Tbsp.
olive oil, salt, and pepper.
Grill for six to eight minutes on each side.
Cut the chicken into bite-size pieces.
In a large bowl combine pasta, romaine lettuce, tomatoes, chicken, croutons, parmesan, and Caesar dressing.
Toss gently.
Enjoy!
