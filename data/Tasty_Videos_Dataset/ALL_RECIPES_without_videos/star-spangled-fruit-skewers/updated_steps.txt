For 3 of the skewers add one strawberry and one banana, alternating colors until you have about 2 inches left on the end of skewer.
To represent the flag, start with a strawberry and end with a strawberry.
Then add 5 blueberries to the end.
Set on the left side of a platter or tray.
For the rest of the skewers, add alternating strawberries and bananas until you run out of room on your skewer, again starting and ending with strawberries.
Set to the right of the other skewers.
Bring to your party, serve, and enjoy!
