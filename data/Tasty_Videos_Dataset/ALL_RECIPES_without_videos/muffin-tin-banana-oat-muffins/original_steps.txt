Preheat oven to 350ºF/180ºC
In a medium-sized bowl, mash a ripened banana until mostly smooth. Add egg and stir
Add honey, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, vanilla and Greek yogurt, and mix.
Fold in the oats and lastly incorporate the milk.
Fill each cup of a greased six-cup muffin tin a quarter of the way full. In each respective cup, add strawberries, apple, blueberries, blackberries, raspberries, and almonds, then fill cups to the top with the rest of batter.
Bake 30 minutes or until risen slightly and turning dark and a bit crunchy on the edges.
Allow to cool for 5 minutes.
Enjoy!
