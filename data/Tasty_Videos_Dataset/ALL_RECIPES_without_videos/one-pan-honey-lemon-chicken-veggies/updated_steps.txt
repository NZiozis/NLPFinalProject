Preheat oven to 400˚F/200˚C.
In a small bowl, combine lemon juice, olive oil, honey, and garlic.
Set aside.
In a cast-iron skillet, brown chicken for 2-3 minutes on each side.
Add olive oil, salt, and pepper.
Remove from pan and set aside.
Add the green beans, cherry tomatoes, and carrots to the pan.
Return chicken to pan on top of the vegetables.
Pour the sauce on top of the chicken and veggies.
Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165˚F/75˚C.
Serve immediately and enjoy!
