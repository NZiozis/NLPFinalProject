Preheat oven to 375˚F (190˚C).
In a small bowl, combine paprika, cayenne, garlic powder, oregano, thyme, and salt.
Reserve 1½ teaspoons of the seasoning.
Set aside.
Coat both sides of the chicken breasts with the seasoning mixture.
In an oiled cast-iron skillet, cook chicken on medium-high heat 4 minutes per side, or until chicken is browned.
Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165˚F (72˚C).
Let the chicken rest for several minutes.
In a small bowl, combine yogurt, minced garlic, lime juice, olive oil and reserved seasoning mixture.
Set aside.
Cut chicken into pieces.
In a large bowl, add lettuce, chicken, red onion, avocado, cherry tomatoes, and black beans.
Add dressing and toss.
Enjoy!
