Place cookies in a resealable bag and crush with a wooden spoon until crumbs.
Set aside
Separate the egg whites into a bowl.
Add in sugar, vanilla, melted butter, and salt.
Mix until combined.
Sift the flour and add it to the bowl along with the cookie crumbs and milk.
NOTE: Add more milk if too thick.
Mix until combined.
Heat a nonstick skillet over a medium heat and pour in a Â¼ cup (60 ml) of batter.
Swirl around the pan until coated.
Cook until the surface begins to bubble and flip.
Cook for another couple of minutes and then transfer to a baking tray.
Quickly roll into a cone shape and hold until set (about a minute).
Cool and top with your favorite ice cream and additional cookie crumbs.
Enjoy!
